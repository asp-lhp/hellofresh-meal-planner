Mozzarella-Cauli Bites & Smashed Potatoes
⏱️ Prep: 20 min 🔥 Cook: 40 min 🍽️ Servings: 2

⚡ Quick Reference

From Fridge: (Recipe details needed)
Cook Fresh: Cauliflower, potatoes
Pantry: Mozzarella, yogurt, dill
Total Time: 60 min

🛒 Ingredients

  • Cauliflower1 head (2 lbs)
  • Baby potatoes1 lb
  • Fresh mozzarella8 oz cubed
  • Haloumi cheese7 oz package
  • Greek yogurt1/2 cup
  • Fresh dill1/4 cup chopped
  • Salad greens4 cups
  • Cherry tomatoes1 cup
  • Cucumber1
  • Panko breadcrumbs1/2 cup
  • Eggs2
  • Lemon, garlic, olive oilTo taste

👨‍🍳 Instructions

1
Roast potatoes. Preheat oven to 425°F. Boil baby potatoes for 15 min until tender. Drain, place on baking sheet. Smash each with a fork, drizzle with olive oil, salt & pepper. Roast 25 min until crispy.
⏱️ 40 min
2
Prep cauliflower bites. Cut cauliflower into florets. Dip in beaten egg, then coat in panko breadcrumbs. Add cubed mozzarella to some florets. Heat oil in a skillet and fry in batches until golden (4-5 min per batch).
⏱️ 15 min
3
Make yogurt dressing. Mix Greek yogurt with minced garlic, chopped dill, lemon juice, salt & pepper. Thin with a bit of water if needed.
⏱️ 3 min
4
Sear haloumi & assemble. Slice haloumi and sear in a hot skillet 2 min per side until golden. Spread yogurt dressing on plates, top with salad greens, smashed potatoes, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, cauliflower bites, and haloumi. Drizzle with hot sauce and dill.
⏱️ 10 min
Cheesy Beef Taco Mac Pasta
⏱️ Prep: 4 min 🔥 Cook: 9 min 🍽️ Servings: 2

⚡ Quick Reference

From Fridge: (Recipe details needed)
Cook Fresh: Ground beef, pasta
Pantry: Taco seasoning, cheese
Total Time: 13 min

🛒 Ingredients

  • Ground beef 12-16 oz
  • Cavatappi pasta 8 oz
  • Sweet bell pepper 1
  • Roma tomato 1
  • Cheddar cheese 1/2 cup shredded
  • Sour cream 1/4 cup
  • Taco seasoning / Enchilada spice 2 tbsp

👨‍🍳 Instructions

1
Boil water for pasta. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook pasta according to package directions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
⏱️ 8-10 min
2
Brown the beef. While pasta cooks, heat a large skillet over medium-high. Add ground beef and cook, breaking up with a spoon, until browned (5-7 min). Drain excess fat.
⏱️ 7 min
3
Add veggies and seasoning. Dice bell pepper and tomato. Add to beef along with taco seasoning. Cook 2-3 minutes until veggies soften.
⏱️ 3 min
4
Combine and serve. Add cooked pasta to skillet with beef mixture. Toss to combine. Remove from heat and stir in cheese. Serve topped with sour cream and green onions.
⏱️ 2 min
Swedish-Inspired Burgers
⏱️ Prep: 25 min 🔥 Cook: 25 min 🍽️ Servings: 2

⚡ Quick Reference

From Fridge: Ground beef, onions, mushrooms
Cook Fresh: Burgers, potato coins, mushroom sauce
Pantry: Breadcrumbs, spices, cream
Total Time: 50 min

🛒 Ingredients

  • Ground beef1 lb
  • Breadcrumbs1/4 cup
  • Egg1
  • Onion1 (half diced, half sliced)
  • Mushrooms8 oz sliced
  • Heavy cream1/2 cup
  • Beef broth1/2 cup
  • Dijon mustard1 tbsp
  • Worcestershire sauce1 tbsp
  • Baby potatoes1 lb
  • Sour cream, dill, lemonFor dilly dip
  • SpicesPaprika, allspice, S&P

👨‍🍳 Instructions

1
Roast potato coins. Preheat oven to 425°F. Slice potatoes into 1/4" coins. Toss with olive oil, salt, pepper, paprika. Roast on baking sheet 25-30 min, flipping halfway, until golden and crispy.
⏱️ 30 min
2
Form burger patties. Mix ground beef with breadcrumbs, egg, half the diced onion, garlic, paprika, allspice, salt & pepper. Form into 4 thin patties. (Thinner = more Swedish-style!)
⏱️ 10 min
3
Cook burgers. Heat 1 tbsp olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high. Cook patties 3-4 min per side until browned and cooked through. Remove and keep warm.
⏱️ 8 min
4
Make mushroom sauce. In the same skillet, add butter. Sauté sliced onions and mushrooms until golden (5 min). Add beef broth, cream, Dijon, Worcestershire. Simmer 5 min until thickened. Season with S&P.
⏱️ 12 min
5
Make dilly dip & serve. Mix sour cream with chopped dill, lemon juice, salt. Serve burgers smothered in mushroom sauce, with potato coins and dilly dip on the side. (Optional: serve on buns)
⏱️ 3 min
Swicy Poke-Inspired Salmon Bowls
⏱️ Prep: 1 min 🔥 Cook: 40 min 🍽️ Servings: 2

⚡ Quick Reference - PREPPED!

From Fridge: "Thu Salmon Bowl Veggies" container
Cook Fresh: Rice (1 cup), Salmon (12 oz)
Toppings: Spicy mayo, sweet soy glaze
Total Time: 25 min
✨ PREP SUNDAY: Your "Thu Salmon Bowl Veggies" container has: radishes, cucumbers, scallions, ginger, slaw, and dressing already mixed!

🛒 Ingredients

  • Salmon fillets 12 oz (2 fillets)
  • White rice 1 cup dry
  • 🟢 Prepped veggie container From fridge
  • Spicy mayo 2-3 tbsp
  • Sweet soy glaze 2-3 tbsp
  • Sesame seeds 1 tbsp

👨‍🍳 Instructions

1
Start rice. Rinse 1 cup white rice. Cook according to package directions (usually 2 cups water, bring to boil, simmer 15-20 min). Fluff with fork when done.
⏱️ 20 min
2
Marinate salmon. Pat salmon dry. Mix together sweet soy glaze with a bit of the minced ginger from your prepped container. Brush on salmon. Let sit 10 min.
⏱️ 10 min
3
Sear salmon. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a large skillet over medium-high. Add salmon skin-side up and cook 4-5 min. Flip and cook another 3-4 min until cooked through and glazed.
⏱️ 8 min
4
Assemble bowls. Divide rice between 2 bowls. Add salmon. Grab your "Thu Salmon Bowl Veggies" container from the fridge and divide between bowls. Drizzle with spicy mayo and remaining soy glaze. Sprinkle with sesame seeds.
⏱️ 3 min

Swicy Poke-Inspired Tilapia Bowls
⏱️ Prep: 1 min 🔥 Cook: 40 min 🍽️ Servings: 2

⚡ Quick Reference - PREPPED!

From Fridge: "Fri Tilapia Bowl Veggies" container
Cook Fresh: Rice (1 cup), Tilapia (frozen)
Toppings: Spicy mayo, sweet soy glaze
Total Time: 25 min
✨ PREP SUNDAY: Your "Fri Tilapia Bowl Veggies" container has: radishes, cucumbers, scallions, ginger, slaw, and dressing already mixed!

🛒 Ingredients

  • Tilapia fillets (frozen) 12 oz (2 fillets)
  • White rice 1 cup dry
  • 🟢 Prepped veggie container From fridge
  • Spicy mayo 2-3 tbsp
  • Sweet soy glaze 2-3 tbsp
  • Sesame seeds 1 tbsp

👨‍🍳 Instructions

1
Start rice. Rinse 1 cup white rice. Cook according to package directions (usually 2 cups water, bring to boil, simmer 15-20 min). Fluff with fork when done.
⏱️ 20 min
2
Prepare tilapia. Thaw frozen tilapia if needed (run under cool water 5 min). Pat very dry. Season with salt and pepper. Brush with sweet soy glaze mixed with ginger from your prepped container.
⏱️ 10 min
3
Cook tilapia. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a large skillet over medium-high. Add tilapia and cook 3-4 min per side until golden and flaky. Brush with more glaze while cooking.
⏱️ 8 min
4
Assemble bowls. Divide rice between 2 bowls. Add tilapia. Grab your "Fri Tilapia Bowl Veggies" container from the fridge and divide between bowls. Drizzle with spicy mayo and remaining soy glaze. Sprinkle with sesame seeds.
⏱️ 3 min
