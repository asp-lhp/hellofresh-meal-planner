💡 Meal Prep Success Tips

  • Prep in order: Start with washing/drying, then chopping, then sauces/marinades
  • Use glass containers: They're better for reheating and keeping food fresh
  • Label everything: Use masking tape and a marker with the day and meal name
  • Don't prep rice yet: Cook rice fresh or use a rice cooker on the day you need it
  • Keep proteins frozen: Thaw salmon TUESDAY night for Wednesday dinner
  • Mon-Tue & Thu meals: No prepped containers - cook fresh using recipe cards
  • Wed & Fri meals: Grab prepped containers and just cook rice + protein!
